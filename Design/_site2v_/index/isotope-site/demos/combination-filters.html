Combination filters

Filters can be combined. In addition to filtering for just .red or .tall, you can pass in a filter selector of both: .red.tall.

Filters

color

  • any
  • red
  • blue
  • yellow

size

  • any
  • small
  • wide
  • tall
  • big

shape

  • any
  • round
  • square
